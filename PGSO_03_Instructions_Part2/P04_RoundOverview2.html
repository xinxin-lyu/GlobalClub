Earnings

Your earning in each round is:

1 x Number of points in your Private Account
+
0.6 x Number of points in Group Account

For example, {{ if HOMO }} if you keep 5 points in your private account and allocate 15 points to the group account, suppose the other three members allocated 30 points to the group account, your earning in this round will be 5 + 0.6*(15+30) = 5 + 0.6*45 = 32 points. {{ else }} suppose you have 10 points as endowment. If you keep 5 points in your private account and allocate 5 points to the group account, suppose the other three members allocated 30 points to the group account, your earning in this round will be 5 + 0.6*(5+30) = 5 + 0.6*35 = 26 points. {{ endif }}

At the end of each round, your earnings for the round will be recorded. Your payment for part 2 will be determined by the sum of the points that you earn in each round before the part ends with a conversion rate of: {{PointsPerDollar}} points = $1.
