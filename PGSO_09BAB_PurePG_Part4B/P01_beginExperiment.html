  1. In this part, you will remain in the same group as in part 2 and 3. You will face the same decision task as in part 2. Each round, you receive the same endowment as before.
  2. Part 4 is made up of 1 match. It contains a number of rounds with a continuation probability of 90%.
  3. Your group will be randomly paired with another group in the room. You will have the opportunity to access to a Group Account #2 by paying a fee that varies across matches.
  4. Each participant must decide:
    • 1) whether to pay the fee and access to Group Account #2 or not
    • 2) how to allocate their points between the Private Account and the Group Account(s)
  5. Each point allocated in your Private Account gives you 1 point.
  6. Each point allocated in the Group Account gives every member 0.6 point.
  7. Each point allocated in the Group Account #2 gives everyone who paid the fee 0.6 point.
