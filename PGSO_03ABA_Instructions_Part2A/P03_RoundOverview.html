Endowment {{ if HOMO }}

At the beginning of each round, you receive 20 points and the other three participants in your group each receive 20 points.

{{ else }}

At the beginning of each round, two of your group members will receive 10 points and the other two will receive 30 points. Who receives how much is randomly determined. However, the endowments stay the same in this part. That means, if you receive 10 points in the first round, you will continue receiving 10 points in each round.

{{ endif }} Decision Task

Everyone in your group has a Private Account and your group of four has a Group Account .

In each round, you need to decide how to allocate your points between the Private Account and the Group Account .

Each point in the Private Account yields a return of 1 point to YOU in that round.

Each point in the Group Account yields a return of 0.6 point to EVERYONE in the group. (Note: everyone in your group can allocate points to the Group Account.)
