Decision Task

Each round has two decision stages.

In stage 1, you need to decide whether to pay a fee of {{FC}} points and gain access to Group Account #2.

In stage 2, you will know who else paid the fee and gained the access to Group Account #2.

  • If you paid the fee, you can allocate the rest of your endowment among three accounts:
    your Private Account, Group Account , and Group Account #2.

Everyone in your group and the other group has a Private Account. Your group of four has a Group Account . Everyone in your group and the other group can pay the fee and access to Group Account #2.

Each point in the Private Account yields a return of 1 point to YOU in that round.

Each point in the Group Account yields a return of 0.6 point to EVERYONE in the group. (Note: everyone in your group can allocate points to the Group Account.)

Each point in the Group Account #2 yields a return of 0.6 point to EVERYONE who paid the fee. (Note: everyone who paid the fee can allocate points to the Group Account #2.)
